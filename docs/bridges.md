# Bridges

Bridges allow users to transfer assets between Ethereum and Base.

## Why Use a Bridge?

Users may want to move assets to Base in order to access lower transaction fees and applications available on the network.

## Common Bridge Types

### Official Bridge

The official bridge is commonly used to transfer assets between Ethereum and Base.

### Third-Party Bridges

Some cross-chain protocols also support transfers to and from Base.

## Important Notes

- Always verify the destination network.
- Double-check wallet addresses before confirming transactions.
- Be aware of bridge fees and confirmation times.
